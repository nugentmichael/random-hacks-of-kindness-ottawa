Random Hacks of Kindness Ottawa

Join us on our two-day Ottawa hackathon on Dec 7-8, 2013.

We will be developing solutions to address challenges faced by non-profits, and other humanitarian organizations.

Projects we will be hacking on.

Random Hacks of Kindness is a global initiative to make the world a better place by developing solutions in response to challenges facing humanity. Check out some of the projects we will be working on during the hackathon.

Calling all designers, front-end and back-end developers!

Come join us in working on interesting projects solving real-world problems. The cost is 5$. We provide the space to hack, 5 meals throughout the weekend and awesome swag.
